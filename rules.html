📋 Challenge Rules

Player Reactivation Challenge — Official Rules & Guidelines

📅 Duration & Entry

  • Challenge period: July 1 – July 31, 2026
  • Each participant receives an approximately equal base of players for reactivation
  • A single player can be reactivated no more than once every 5 days
Entry fee: 10 Kudos

📈 Leaderboard

  • The leaderboard is updated once per day

🎯 NGR Target

  • The combined NGR from all reactivations must reach a minimum of 100,000 NGR
  • If the total NGR is below the target — all challenge results are voided

🚫 Do Not Reactivate

  • Negative-balance players
  • Players with a high bonus rate
⚠️ Penalty: −5,000 NGR deducted for reactivating such a player

Exception

  • Players with 1–2 deposits under a welcome bonus are allowed

⚡ Activity & Conduct

  • If a manager does not begin reactivation work within the first week, their player base is seized and redistributed among active participants
  • Using inadequate or abusive offers results in automatic disqualification from the challenge

🎰 Special Case — Big Win

If a manager successfully reactivates a qualifying player who then hits a big win, only 50% of that player's deposit amount is counted toward the manager's result.

📊 Scoring Methodology

Player Tagging

Each manager must tag their reactivated players with:

react_vip_[your_nickname]

Record Keeping

  • After tagging, the manager must log the player's ID in the tracking spreadsheet:
    Open Tracking Sheet →
  • All reactivated players are logged in a dedicated sheet under each manager's tab

Quality Review

  • The Quality team verifies that each player was genuinely reactivated by the claiming manager
  • Players who were already active without the manager's involvement are not counted

🏆 Final Results

  • Results are calculated based on NGR data from Holistics
  • After the challenge ends, a 2-day review period is provided for:
    • Correcting potential errors
    • Reviewing participant appeals
